Galactic ASKAP Spectral Line Survey

GASKAP-HI

A minimal project home for neutral hydrogen observations, data products, science updates, and collaboration resources.

About

GASKAP-HI focuses on neutral hydrogen science from ASKAP, tracing Galactic structure, gas dynamics, and the interstellar medium at high angular resolution.

This site is inspired by the clear, astronomy-focused structure of the POSSUM survey website. Images and richer visual material can be added later.

Science

Science themes include Galactic HI emission, absorption, cold neutral medium studies, cloud structure, and links between atomic gas and star formation.
