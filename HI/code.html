IViS

IViS (Interferometric Visibility-domain Inversion Software) is a fast, GPU-accelerated non-linear deconvolution tool for radio interferometry. It is designed for large mosaics and hyperspectral cubes, with a focus on lowering the energy footprint of large imaging tasks.

IViS was developed in the context of GASKAP-HI as a complementary imaging software to ASKAPSoft for producing emission cubes. Its regularized, MEM-like framework is especially suited to diffuse emission, and currently supports joint deconvolution through image-plane mosaicking with direction-dependent primary-beam effects.

IViS is a generic, flexible framework for developing new deconvolution methods and is compatible with MeasurementSet v2 data.
